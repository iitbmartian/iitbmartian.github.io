GAURAV RATHOD

Meet Gaurav, our most fun-loving Team Leads at MRT. Having a fathomless Aero enthu, he's super passionate about rover interplanetary missions. From his experience in Gulfstream and DRDO, he is the lead mechanic of our team, whose dextrous fingers can tighten the minuest of bolts. His never-say-die attitude and leading by showing makes him a chill, fun guy for whom sky is not the limit, it is just the beginning!!.

KHUSH JAIN

Meet Khush Jain, our exceptional Team Lead at MRT. His expertise in AI, Robotics, and Mathematics, combined with his previous roles as an Adobe Research intern and Head of Autonomous Systems, makes him the driving force behind our team's success. Join us on this journey of innovation with Khush!.

HARSH SHAH

Meet Harsh Shah, the leader of our Team from the Computer Science and Engineering Department. Harsh excels in vision, robotics, and system design. He drives innovation with projects like object detection, GPS apps, and autonomous navigation. His dedication to optimizing rover traversal unites our team. Stay tuned for more from Harsh Shah and the MRT team!.
